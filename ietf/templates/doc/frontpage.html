About this site

The IETF Datatracker is the IETF's web system for managing organizational information, such as:

  • Internet-Drafts (I-Ds) and Requests for Comments (RFCs)
  • IPR disclosures
  • Liaison statements
  • Meeting agendas and session agendas, slides and minutes
  • IESG telechat agendas
