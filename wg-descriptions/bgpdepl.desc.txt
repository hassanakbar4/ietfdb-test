The major purpose of this group is to coordinate BGP deployment and
application in the current Internet.
 
It intends to create a forum for BGP users to share BGP deployment
experiences and also provide a channel for users to communicate with
router vendors who implemented or who are implementing BGP.  It also intends
to discuss BGP policy application and coordinate policy implementation
in the current Internet routing environment which includes defining the
usage of policy, defining a mechanism to share policy information, etc.
