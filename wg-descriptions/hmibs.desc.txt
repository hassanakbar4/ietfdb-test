Objectives of BOF:
1. To consider the creation of a new IETF working group called
*H.Series Multimedia MIBs*. The proposed scope for this working group
includes the definition of managed objects for: H.323 Terminals, H.323
Gatekeepers, H.323 MCUs; H.320 Terminals, H.320 MCUs and H.323/H.320
Gateways.  Other items under consideration could be the definition of
managed objects for H.323/PSTN Gateways.
2. To develop the charter for this new working group
3. To create the list of candidate Internet-Drafts which will comprise
the MIB hierarchy for Multimedia Terminals and Systems
4. To develop Milestones and Work Schedule - 15 minutes
5. To discuss relationships with ITU-T  - 15 minutes
